Post Parade Information

Band Camp Information

  • Band Camp is August 4-8 at "the BEAST" (2900 Sunflower Rd.)
  • Registration begins at 12:15 on the 4th
  • Bring your instrument and camp registration fee of $40
  • We accept cash, check (made out to SHS Band), or credit/debit card
  • Rehearsal is from 12:30-4:30pm
  • Band Camp Itinerary - Coming Soon

General Announcements

  • Everyone: Please wash your uniform on a gentle cycle with cold water, and dry it on low heat.
  • Non-Fall Marchers: Please return your uniform on July 28th between 7:30 and 8:30 AM in the SHS band room. You can come at any time during that hour, and the process should only take about 5 minutes.

Thank you for a great Summer!
